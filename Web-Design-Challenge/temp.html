Max Temperature

Look at the data here we see that there is a correlation between Max Temperature and Latitute. As we can see from the picture there Max Temperture peaks at ~80 to 90 F, which is around -10 to 10 Latitude. Then pass 10 Latitude the temperature drops as the Latitude rises.
